About me

Software Developer / Electrical Engineer / Firefighter

Keith Huster

ASP.NET Full-Stack Developer / Software Engineer

A well-respected ASP.NET Full-Stack Developer / Software Engineering Specialist capable of taking ideas from concept to production. Keith has over 13 years of experience developing both pure software systems as well as embedded electro-mechanical systems for the education and medical device markets.

Keith currently holds 13 US patents for various medical devices and software systems. Keith is also a volunteer firefighter who enjoys cycling, snow skiing, and camping.
